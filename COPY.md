## Onboarding Flow (/start)

### Step 1: Entry
**Headline:** Let's figure out what's holding you back.
**Sub:** Takes 60 seconds. No email required.
**CTA:** Start -->

### Step 2: Business Context
**Question:** What best describes your business?
**Options:**
- Service business (consulting, agency, professional services)
- Product / E-commerce
- SaaS / Tech startup
- Local business (retail, hospitality, wellness)
- Creator / Personal brand
- Other

### Step 3: Growth Stage
**Question:** Where are you right now?
**Options:**
- Just starting out — need to look legitimate
- Growing — need to scale what's working
- Established — need to modernise / optimise
- Stuck — something's broken and I don't know what

### Step 4: What Feels Broken
**Question:** What's costing you the most right now?
**Options (multi-select):**
- My website doesn't generate leads
- I can't keep up with content
- Too much manual/admin work
- I know AI could help but don't know where to start
- I'm paying too many different vendors
- Honestly? All of it.

### Step 5: The Diagnosis
**Headline:** Here's what we see.

*Personalised based on selections:*

A visual "health check" card with bars/indicators for:
- **Website** — Strong / Needs Work / Critical
- **Content** — Strong / Needs Work / Critical
- **Operations** — Strong / Needs Work / Critical

Each bar links to the relevant service arm with a one-line prescription.

**Example for someone who selected "website doesn't generate leads" + "can't keep up with content":**

> Your website is the foundation — but it's not pulling its weight. And without consistent content driving traffic to it, even a great site underperforms.
>
> **Our recommendation:** Start with a strategic website rebuild, then layer in a content retainer to keep the momentum going.

**Investment Estimate:**
Below the diagnosis, show a rough investment range based on their selections:
> Based on what you've told us, businesses like yours typically invest **$X,000 - $X,000** to fix what's broken. That includes [relevant services].

This gives value before asking for contact info and anchors the pricing conversation.

### Step 6: Book the Call
**Headline:** We already know where to start.
**Sub:** Let's talk about [their specific gap — dynamically inserted].
**CTA:** Book a 15-Minute Call (Calendly embed)
**Skip:** "I'll explore the site first" --> redirect to homepage

### Flow Notes (Implementation)
- Auto-save progress (localStorage) so returning visitors resume, don't restart
- Conditional logic: if they select "Just starting out" + "Service business", skip content questions and weight toward website
- "Honestly? All of it." selection should trigger the holistic package recommendation
- After booking/skipping, full site renders with subtle emphasis on their identified service areas
- No email gate at any point in the flow — the Calendly embed captures contact info naturally
