How SA businesses actually find IT providers

This is different from the US/UK. In SA, trust comes before transaction. The digital journey validates, it doesn't originate.

The SA B2B buying sequence: Referral/word-of-mouth (discovery) -- Google search (validation) -- Website visit (credibility check) -- WhatsApp/call (first contact) -- Face-to-face meeting (relationship) -- Close.

1
Referrals & Word of Mouth
84% of B2B buyers start with a referral. Ubuntu culture drives business relationships. WhatsApp business groups are where referrals actually happen. Referral leads convert 30% better than other channels.
84% start here
2
Google (Search + Maps + GBP)
93% of SA consumers search locally online. Google is the validation layer -- people search after getting a referral. 88% of mobile local searches lead to action within 24 hours. GBP is critical for local services.
93% validate here
3
LinkedIn
7 million SA professionals. 85% engagement rate (above global average). 80% of B2B social leads come from LinkedIn. Lead gen forms convert at 13% (5x industry average). Founder content outperforms company pages.
80% of B2B leads
4
WhatsApp
94% of SA's digital population. 98% open rate (vs 20% for email). 27% conversion rate. Not just a messaging app -- it's the primary business communication channel in SA. Response speed directly correlates with close rate.
98% open rate
5
Google Ads (PPC)
SA CPC is 3-5x cheaper than US/UK. IT services keywords R8-R50/click. High-intent queries ("IT support Johannesburg") convert well. Best ROI when paired with a strong landing page.
R8-R50 CPC
6
Face-to-Face / Networking
In-person is not optional in SA -- it's the price of entry for high-value contracts. First meetings build rapport, not pitch. Digital feeds the pipeline, the sales guy closes in person.
Closes deals
